Below is some useful information links for each council area which should assist development managers working on the Supporting Communities programme and beyond.  It is aimed to be a collection of useful links to reduce time spent searching and speed up the process of community mapping and research.  It is a fluid document and links may break or change – or others may find that additional information may be useful – in these instances users are asked to update the document where inaccurate/broken links are found and add where beneficial.
